About Me

My name is Will Barnes. I am a graduate student at Rice University in the Department of Physics & Astronomy studying plasma dynamics in the upper solar atmosphere.

My interests include building Raspberry Pi computing clusters, rabidly promoting good development practice in scientific computing, and finding new ways to grind, pour, sip, and brew coffee.
